Welcome, fellow Resistance agent!



You have joined the winning side in the war against the mysterious, alien Shapers who seek to control our minds and supress the human spirit. Your fellow Resistance agents fight to preserve individuality and free will against the hive mind that the Shapers and their so-called Enlightened minions seek to impose upon humanity. The fate of our world is in your hands!

Fellow Resistance agents in the Chicago area are here to help you improve your battle skills and assist you in gathering the equipment you will need to combat the Enlightened. You are not alone in this fight!



Protect humanity from the Enlightened oppressors! Resistance is essential.
